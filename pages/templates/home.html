Introduction

NRIT Ltd is a Natural Resource and Information Technology consultancy firm with headquarters in Nairobi, Kenya. It is registered under the Companies Act (Cap. 486) as a limited company. It focuses on provisioning technical services to support improved management of Natural Resources using best practices. It has a pool of experts from various fields of natural resource management some on full time while others are on short notice call.

Vission

To be a lead consultancy firm in natural resource information and technology development.

Mission

To offer our customers quality and timely services that enhances their capacity to improve management of natural resources
